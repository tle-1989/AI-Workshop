üìù Breaking Down Complex Assignments with Goblin Tools

Scenario: Research Paper Assignment

Challenge: Many students feel overwhelmed by multi-week research projects.

Solution: Use Goblin Tools' Magic ToDo to break down "Write 10-page research paper" into manageable steps.

Example Output:

  • Choose and narrow topic (2 days)
  • Find 5 scholarly sources (3 days)
  • Create outline (1 day)
  • Write introduction (2 days)
  • Draft body paragraphs (1 week)
  • Write conclusion (1 day)
  • Revise and edit (3 days)

Faculty Tip: Demonstrate this live in class, then ask students to use it for their own projects.
